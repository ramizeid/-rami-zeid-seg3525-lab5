Our Services

Acupuncture

Acupuncture is a stimulation therapy that involves inserting very fine needles into specific points in the body. It is an ancient Chinese remedy that encourages natural healing and pain relief by promoting harmony within the body.

Price: $15/Hour

Chinese Cupping

Chinese Cupping is an antient alternative therapy that is used to treat a wide variety of conditions that create muscle aches and pains. During treatment, a cup is placed on the skin and then suctioned onto the skin, which helps with pain and inflammation as well as facilitate healing with blood flow.

Price: $25/Hour

Massage Therapy

Massage Therapy is a hands-on manipulation of the soft tissue and joints of the body conducted by our top of the line massage therapists. The soft tissues include muscle, skin, tendon and associated fascia, ligaments and joint capsules.

Price: $30/Hour

Concussion Therapy

Our interdisciplinary team of healthcare professionals will work with you and your Doctor or specialist to complete a timely assessment and develop a customized treatment plan to address your specific health concerns. We provide an advanced approach to concussion awareness and management to ensure that you experience the least amount of symptoms.

Price: $45/Hour

Electrotherapy

Electrotherapy is a more recent evolution of energy-based physiotherapy techniques. In this form of treatment, electrical stimulation is provided by attaching electrodes to the skin, which helps stimulate the body's muscles.

Price: $50/Hour
